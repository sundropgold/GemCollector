COLLECT THE GEMS!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random generated number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each gem is hidden until you discover it by clicking on it!

Each time when the game starts, you'll be given a new random number to match, and each gem's value changes.
